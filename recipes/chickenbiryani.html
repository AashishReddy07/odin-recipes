Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a delicious Pakistani/Indian rice dish that's typically reserved for special occasions such as weddings, parties, or holidays such as Ramadan.

It has a lengthy preparation, but the work is definitely worth it. For biryani, basmati rice is the ideal variety to use.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless chicken pieces cut into chunks
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large onions,finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 pound basmati rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat 4 tablespoons of oil and add onions, chilli powder, salt. Let it cook for 5 min
  3. Add chicken and cook until it is tender for about 35 to 45 minutes
  4. Wash the rice and cook it. Add it to the chicken pieces after the rice is cooked
  5. Stir rice and chicken together and serve it with some onions
