Mutton Biryani

Description

Biryani is a bit of a project and is time-consuming, but I have never been disappointed with the results.

Since it is such a festive dish, I usually make it for dinner parties. It is a huge hit.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds boneless, skinless lamb pieces cut into chunks
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large onions,finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 1 pound basmati rice
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat 4 tablespoons of oil and add onions, chilli powder, salt. Let it cook for 5 min
  3. Add mutton pieces and cook until it is tender for about 35 to 45 minutes
  4. Wash the rice and cook it. Add it to the mutton pieces after the rice is cooked
  5. Stir rice and mutton together and serve it with some onions
